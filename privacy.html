Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters to Us

At Lux Med Concierge, LLC, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our services.

Last Updated: February 26, 2025

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide you with our telehealth services:

  • Personal Information: Name, date of birth, contact details (email, phone number, address), and payment information.
  • Health Information: Medical history, current medications, allergies, and other health-related information necessary for treatment.
  • Communication Data: Records of your interactions with our healthcare providers through telehealth consultations.
  • Website Usage: Information about how you use our website, collected through cookies and similar technologies.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Providing Healthcare Services: To deliver personalized healthcare services, including consultations, treatment plans, and medication management.
  • Communication: To communicate with you about your care, appointments, and treatment options.
  • Billing and Administration: To process payments and manage our business operations.
  • Improvement of Services: To analyze and improve our services, website functionality, and user experience.

HIPAA Compliance

As a healthcare provider, we are committed to complying with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). This means we:

  • Implement physical, technical, and administrative safeguards to protect your health information
  • Limit access to your health information to authorized personnel only
  • Obtain your consent before sharing your information with third parties, except as required by law
  • Provide you with access to your health information upon request

Sharing of Information

We may share your information with:

  • Healthcare Providers: Other healthcare professionals involved in your care.
  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that help us operate our business, such as payment processors and shipping services.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, such as in response to a court order or legal process.

Your Rights

Under HIPAA and other applicable laws, you have the right to:

Access Your Information

Request to see and obtain a copy of your health records.

Amend Your Information

Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete information.

Accounting of Disclosures

Request a list of when and to whom your health information was shared.

Request Restrictions

Ask us to limit the information we share about you.

Security Measures

We implement robust security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data during transmission and storage
  • Secure telehealth platforms that comply with HIPAA requirements
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Staff training on privacy and security best practices

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website and/or sending you an email notification.
