Master Antioxidant

Glutathione

Glutathione is a powerful antioxidant that is naturally produced in the body. It plays a critical role in protecting cells from oxidative stress, detoxifying harmful substances, and supporting immune function. It's often referred to as the "master antioxidant" because it helps to neutralize free radicals and reactive oxygen species (ROS) that can cause damage to cells, proteins, and DNA.

Benefits of Glutathione

  • Antioxidant: Helps protect your cells from oxidative stress, damage and free radicals
  • Detox Support: Helps breakdown and remove toxins/waste products that can accumulate in the body
  • Boost Immune Function: Helps white blood cells work effectively, which is crucial for defending the body against infections
  • Promote Healthy Skin: Helps to reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles by combating oxidative stress
  • Reduce Inflammation: May help lower inflammation, contributing to better recovery and wellness
  • Increased Energy and Mental Clarity: Increased energy and mental clarity to combat fatigue and cognitive decline
  • Muscle Recovery: Supports faster muscle recovery and minimizes oxidative damage to tissues

Energy Booster

B12

B12 injections are a form of Vitamin B12 supplementation given intramuscularly. Vitamin B12 (cobalamin) is an essential vitamin that plays a key role in brain function, nerve health, red blood cell production, and DNA synthesis. B12 shots are taken to address deficiencies or to boost energy levels, as low levels of B12 can lead to fatigue, weakness, and mood changes.

Potential Benefits of B12 Injections

  • Increased energy levels and combat fatigue
  • Enhanced mood and reduced symptoms of depression
  • Improved cognitive function and mental clarity
  • Support for metabolic processes, potentially aiding in weight management
  • Essential in the production of healthy red blood cells
  • Support for cardiovascular health by helping lower homocysteine levels

Metabolism Booster

MIC B12

MIC B12 injections are a combination of three main components: Methionine, Inositol, Choline, and Vitamin B12. Each of these plays a distinct role in supporting metabolism, energy production, and overall well-being. MIC B12 injections are most effective when combined with a healthy diet and regular exercise.

What's in a MIC B12 Injection?

  • B12 is crucial for energy production, metabolism, and red blood cell formation
  • Methionine is an amino acid that helps break down fat in the liver and supports liver function
  • Inositol is a B-complex vitamin that helps break down fat and regulates serotonin
  • Choline assists the liver in processing fats and other waste products

Potential Benefits of MIC B12 Injections

  • Boosting fat metabolism
  • Increasing energy levels
  • Supporting a healthy metabolism
  • Improving mood and focus
  • Promoting liver health
