Why Choose Us for MIC B12 Injections?

At Lux Med Concierge, our MIC B12 injections give your energy and metabolism a boost. This special mix has Methionine, Inositol, and Choline – which helps your body metabolize fat– plus Vitamin B12, which is great for energy.

Here's what we offer:

  • More Energy: Say goodbye to feeling tired and hello to better focus.
  • Better Fat Burning: Helps you manage your weight and burn fat.
  • Care Just For You: We make sure each treatment fits what you need.

Whether you're trying to get healthier or just want to feel more energized, we can help you feel your best.

Who Should Skip These Shots?

MIC B12 injections are usually safe, but not for everyone. Tell us if you have any health problems before getting the shots.

Don't get MIC B12 if:

  • You're allergic to Vitamin B12.
  • You have Leber's disease.
  • You have liver or kidney issues.
  • You're pregnant or breastfeeding.

Your health comes first. We'll complete a medical screening to see if MIC B12 shots are a good move for you.
