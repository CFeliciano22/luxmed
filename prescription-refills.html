Same-Day Prescription Refills

Hassle-Free Process

Electronic Prescription Refills

At Lux Med Concierge, we understand that life gets busy. That's why we offer a convenient way to get your medication refills without the hassle of scheduling appointments or waiting at the doctor's office.

When To Use Our Refill Service

  • Running low on medications like blood pressure medication, oral diabetic medication, or cholesterol medication?
  • Recently moved and haven't found a primary care doctor yet?
  • Need a refill but can't get an appointment with your doctor soon enough?
  • Want to avoid waiting rooms and potential exposure to illness?

Benefits of Our Electronic Refill Service

  • Same-day service - no waiting
  • No appointment needed
  • Convenient from the comfort of your home
  • Electronic prescriptions sent directly to your pharmacy of choice
  • Access to healthcare professionals for medication questions
Important Note

Our electronic prescription refill service is designed for maintenance medications. We cannot prescribe or refill controlled substances. If you have questions about whether your medication qualifies, please contact us.

Simple Process

How It Works

Our electronic prescription refill process is quick and straightforward.

1. Contact Us

Reach out to us via phone, text, WhatsApp, or email with your medication details and pharmacy information.

2. Verification

Our healthcare providers will review your information and verify your prescription needs.

3. Same-Day Delivery

Your prescription is electronically sent to your pharmacy for same-day pickup.

How Do I Get Started?

Contact Lux Med Concierge, LLC via phone, text, WhatsApp or email. Fill out the Patient Information form and a provider will reach out to you shortly!
